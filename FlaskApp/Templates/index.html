Spotify Global Daily 50 Mix Analysis

Welcome to our final project! We are excited to show you our work and analysis regarding Spotify's Daily Global Top 50 Music.

This project focuses on Spotify's Daily Global Top 50 playlist. The dataset is a collection of the playlist's daily songs over a duration of 10 days. This accumulated to our dataset being a total of 500 songs.
